UNESCO EXHIBIT

This project is part of a larger celebration organized by UNESCO, an esteemed international organization dedicated to preserving the world's cultural and natural treasures. By creating this miniature model, the team is not only showcasing the architectural and scenic wonders of Puerto Princesa but also raising awareness about the importance of safeguarding such remarkable places for future generations.
